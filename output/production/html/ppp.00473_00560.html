TO A PRESIDENT.

ALL you are doing and saying is to America dangled  
 mirages; You have not learn'd of Nature—of the politics of  
 Nature, you have not learn'd the great ampli- 
 tude, rectitude, impartiality; You have not seen that only such as they are for These  
 States, And that what is less than they, must sooner or later  
 lift off from These States.
