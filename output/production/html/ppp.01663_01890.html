TESTS.

ALL submit to them where they sit, inner, secure, unapproachable  
 to analysis in the soul, Not traditions, not the outer authorities are the judges, They are the judges of outer authorities and of all traditions, They corroborate as they go only whatever corroborates them- 
 selves, and touches themselves; For all that, they have it forever in themselves to corroborate far  
 and near without one exception.
