TO A COMMON PROSTITUTE.

BE composed—be at ease with me—I am Walt Whitman, liberal  
 and lusty as Nature, Not till the sun excludes you do I exclude you, Not till the waters refuse to glisten for you and the leaves to rustle  
 for you, do my words refuse to glisten and rustle for you.   [ begin page 300 ]ppp.00707.308.jpg My girl I appoint with you an appointment, and I charge you that  
 you make preparation to be worthy to meet me, And I charge you that you be patient and perfect till I come. Till then I salute you with a significant look that you do not forget  
 me.
