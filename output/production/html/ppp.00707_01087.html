GRAND IS THE SEEN.

GRAND is the seen, the light, to me—grand are the sky and  
 stars, Grand is the earth, and grand are lasting time and space, And grand their laws, so multiform, puzzling, evolutionary; But grander far the unseen soul of me, comprehending, endow- 
 ing all those, Lighting the light, the sky and stars, delving the earth, sailing  
 the sea, (What were all those, indeed, without thee, unseen soul? of  
 what amount without thee?) More evolutionary, vast, puzzling, O my soul! More multiform far—more lasting thou than they.
