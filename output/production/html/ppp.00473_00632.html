QUICKSAND YEARS THAT WHIRL ME  
 I KNOW NOT WHITHER.

QUICKSAND years that whirl me I know not whither, Your schemes, politics, fail—lines give way—substan- 
 ces mock and elude me; Only the theme I sing, the great and strong-possess'd  
 soul, eludes not; One's-self, must never give way—that is the final sub- 
 stance—that out of all is sure; Out of politics, triumphs, battles, death—what at last  
 finally remains? When shows break up, what but One's-Self is sure?
