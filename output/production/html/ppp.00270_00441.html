Germs.

FORMS, qualities, lives, humanity, language, thoughts, The ones known, and the ones unknown—the ones on  
 the stars, The stars themselves, some shaped, others unshaped, Wonders as of those countries—the soil, trees, cities,  
 inhabitants, whatever they may be, Splendid suns, the moons and rings, the countless com- 
 binations and effects; Such-like, and as good as such-like, visible here or any- 
 where, stand provided for in a handful of space,  
 which I extend my arm and half enclose with my  
 hand; That contains the start of each and all—the virtue, the  
 germs of all.
