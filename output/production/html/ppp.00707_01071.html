BRAVO, PARIS EXPOSITION!

ADD to your show, before you close it, France, With all the rest, visible, concrete, temples, towers, goods, ma- 
 chines and ores, Our sentiment wafted from many million heart-throbs, ethereal  
 but solid, (We grand-sons and great-grand-sons do not forget your grand- 
 sires,) From fifty Nations and nebulous Nations, compacted, sent over- 
 sea to-day, America's applause, love, memories and good-will.
