SHUT NOT YOUR DOORS TO ME 
  PROUD LIBRARIES.

SHUT not your doors to me, proud libraries, For that which was lacking among you all, yet needed  
 most, I bring; A book I have made for your dear sake, O soldiers, And for you, O soul of man, and you, love of comrades; The words of my book nothing, the life of it every- 
 thing; A book separate, not link'd with the rest, nor felt by  
 the intellect; But you will feel every word, O Libertad! arm'd  
 Libertad! It shall pass by the intellect to swim the sea, the air, With joy with you, O soul of man.
