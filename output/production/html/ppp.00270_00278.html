BEGINNING MY STUDIES.

BEGINNING my studies, the first step pleas'd me so  
 much, The mere fact, consciousness—these forms—the power  
 of motion, The least insect or animal—the senses—eyesight—  
 love; The first step, I say, aw'd me and pleas'd me so much, I have hardly gone, and hardly wish'd to go, any far- 
 ther, But stop and loiter all the time, to sing it in extatic  
 songs.
