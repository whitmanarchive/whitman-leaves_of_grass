 on the stars, The stars themselves, some shaped, others unshaped, Wonders as of those countries—the soil, trees, cities, 
 inhabitants, whatever they may be, Splendid suns, the moons and rings, the countless  
 combinations and effects, Such-like, and as good as such-like, visible here or  
 anywhere, stand provided for in a handful of  
 space, which I extend my arm and half enclose
