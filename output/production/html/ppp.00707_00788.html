FULL OF LIFE NOW.

FULL of life now, compact, visible, I, forty years old the eighty-third year of the States,   [ begin page 112 ]ppp.00707.120.jpg To one a century hence or any number of centuries hence, To you yet unborn these, seeking you. When you read these I that was visible am become invisible, Now it is you, compact, visible, realizing my poems, seeking me, Fancying how happy you were if I could be with you and become  
 your comrade; Be it as if I were with you. (Be not too certain but I am now  
 with you.)
