SAVANTISM.

THITHER, as I look, I see each result and glory re- 
 tracing itself and nestling close, always obli- 
 gated; Thither hours, months, years—thither trades, com- 
 pacts, establishments, even the most minute, Thither every-day life, speech, utensils, politics, per- 
 sons, estates, Thither we also, I with my leaves and songs, trustful,  
 admirant, As a father, to his father going, takes his children  
 along with him.
