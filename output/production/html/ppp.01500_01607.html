1WHAT ship, puzzled at sea, cons for the true reck- 
 oning? Or, coming in, to avoid the bars, and follow the chan- 
 nel, a perfect pilot needs? Here, sailor! Here, ship! take aboard the most per- 
 fect pilot, Whom, in a little boat, putting off, and rowing, I,  
 hailing you, offer. 2What place is besieged, and vainly tries to raise the  
 siege? Lo! I send to that place a commander, swift, brave,  
 immortal, And with him horse and foot—and parks of artillery, And artillerymen, the deadliest that ever fired gun.
