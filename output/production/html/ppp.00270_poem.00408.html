I SAW OLD GENERAL AT BAY.

I SAW old General at bay; (Old as he was, his grey eyes yet shone out in battle  
 like stars;) His small force was now completely hemm'd in, in his  
 works; He call'd for volunteers to run the enemy's lines—a  
 desperate emergency; I saw a hundred and more step forth from the ranks—  
 but two or three were selected; I saw them receive their orders aside—they listen'd  
 with care—the adjutant was very grave; I saw them depart with cheerfulness, freely risking their  
 lives.
